"রং চা"(Rong Cha)- Lemon Tea/ Green Tea

"রং চা"

In Bangladesh, lemon tea or green tea is generally known as "lebur cha" or “rong cha”. The term "lebur" refers to lemon and “rong” refers to colored in Bengali, and adding lemon to tea is a common practice to give it a citrusy and refreshing flavor. Lemon tea is often prepared by adding freshly squeezed lemon juice to black tea, green tea, or herbal tea. It is a popular choice for those who enjoy a zesty twist to their tea. Like other types of tea, it can be customized with additional ingredients such as honey or spices, based on personal preferences.

Rong Cha, or Lemon Tea, is a citrusy symphony that refreshes the mind and tantalizes the taste buds. It's a burst of brightness in every sip.
