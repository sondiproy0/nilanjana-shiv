"আদা চা"(Ada Cha)- Ginger Tea

"আদা চা"

In Bangladesh, ginger tea is commonly known as "ada cha". The term "ada" refers to ginger in Bengali. Ginger tea is a popular beverage in the region and is enjoyed for its warm and spicy flavor, as well as its potential health benefits. Similar to turmeric tea, ginger tea may be prepared with various spices and can be served with or without milk, depending on personal preferences.

Ada Cha, or Ginger Tea, is a zesty infusion that invigorates the senses. It's the remedy for a cold day or a tired soul.
